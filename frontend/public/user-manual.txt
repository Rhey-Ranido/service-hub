Website User Manual (Simple Text)

1) Home / Landing
- What you see: Overview, navigation to search services or sign in.
- What to do: Click "Services" or use the search bar to find providers.

2) Service Search
- What you see: A list/grid of services with filters (category, location, etc.).
- What to do:
  - Type a keyword and/or set your location filters.
  - Click a service card to view its details.

3) Service Details
- What you see: Service description, images, pricing, location, reviews.
- What to do:
  - Read details and browse images.
  - If you want to contact the provider or leave a review, you’ll need to log in or sign up.

4) Login / Signup
- What you see: Forms to sign in or create an account.
- What to do:
  - New user: Fill in the signup form and submit.
  - Returning user: Enter your credentials and sign in.
- After success: You’ll be redirected to your Dashboard.

5) Dashboard
- What you see: Overview of your account, shortcuts to key actions.
- What to do:
  - Post a new service (for providers).
  - Open messages to chat with other users/providers.
  - Go to profile/settings to update your info.

6) Post Service (Providers)
- What you see: A form to create a new service.
- What to do:
  - Fill in title, description, category, pricing, location.
  - Upload one or more images.
  - Submit to publish the service.

7) Manage Services (Providers)
- What you see: List of your services with options to edit or update images.
- What to do:
  - Edit service details or update images.
  - Change service availability/status as needed.

8) Messages
- What you see: Your conversations with other users/providers.
- What to do:
  - Open a conversation to read and reply.
  - Start a new conversation from a service details page (requires login).

9) Profile / Settings
- What you see: Your profile information and account settings.
- What to do:
  - Update name, contact info, and profile image.
  - Adjust preferences (e.g., dark mode if available).
  - Save changes to keep your profile up to date.

10) Logout
- What you see: A simple action to sign out.
- What to do:
  - Click "Logout" from the navigation or settings.
  - You’ll return to the Home/Landing page.

Quick Tips
- You can browse and read service details without logging in.
- You must log in to message providers, post services, and manage your listings.
- Use filters in Service Search to quickly narrow results (e.g., by location or category).
- Keep your profile up to date for better trust and visibility.

Where to Access This Manual
- Open /user-manual.txt directly from the site (e.g., http://localhost:5173/user-manual.txt during local development).
